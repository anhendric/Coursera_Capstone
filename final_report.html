Predicting the best place to open a restaurant in Miami¶

1. Introduction¶

1.1. Background¶

Miami is a coastal metropolis located in Miami-Dade County in southeastern Florida, United States. It is the forty second largest city in the United States. Miami, however, is one of the core cities in a metropolitan area which is the third most populous metropolis on the East coast of the United States, and it is the seventh largest in the country.

Miami being a major center and leader in finance, commerce, culture, arts, and international trade, there is a big mixity in this city, making it a great place for opportunities like opening a new restaurant. With more than 50 new restaurants opened in the previous 12 months, despite the Covid-19 pandemic, Miami sort of becomes the new eden garden of customers ! Indeed, Miami restaurants, which have been allowed to operate at 100 percent capacity since October, are packed. And guests all over the city are having a wild time, enjoying the great weather and the incredible feeling of the wonderful city.

1.2. Problem description¶

So Miami seems to be the perfect city to open a new restaurant. But how can one be sure the kind of restaurant he wants to open is a good idea ? And how should we find the best place to open it ? Let's say, for example, that an italian brand want to open a new italian restaurant in Miami. After a study of the city, it looks like the most succesfull italian restaurant is a pizza place located in Park West, being always full of people. The best idea will then be to open another italian restaurant, in a similar neighborhood of Park West, but also being sure than the density of italian restaurant in this neighborhood is as small as possible. Anyway, with 25 neighborhoods in Miami, some of them being composed of sub-neighborhoods, it can be quite complicated to find the best place. This is why we propose here an AI-assisted analysis to find the best place to open a new commerce, according to the type of venues one wants to open.


1.3. Interests¶

Obviously, a lot of people and brands could be interested in our methodology, as long as it can be quickly translated to any kind of venues (restaurant, fitness club, hospital, clothes shop, ...) in any neighborhood. In fact, anyone who want to open a something new in Miami should include this kind of research in a market study.

2. Data description¶

To answer our main question, we will need data about the city of Miami and, of course, the availables venues in the city. This will be done using

  • BeautifulSoup to get the name, population, superficy and coordinates of the neighborhood in Miami. This will be done by webscrapping this wikipedia page;
  • pgeocode to get the longitude and latitude values of the Miami City;
  • foursquare to get the venues available in each neighborhoods;
  • scikit-learn to train a clustering method;
  • folium to be able to view our results on the map of Toronto.

3. Methodology¶

3.1. Acquiring venues of each neighborhood¶

Now that we have the complete list of all the neighborhood in Miami, we are going to use Foursquare API to the the list of the venues, creating a function to list venues for a particular list of neighborhoods, according to the center and global radius of each neighborhood. In order to get the radius of each neighborhood, we're going to suppose each neighborhood is a circle, with the previously computed area. The radius, converted into meters, can then be calculated using

\begin{equation} \text{radius} = 1000*\sqrt{\frac{\text{Area}}{\pi}}. \end{equation}
